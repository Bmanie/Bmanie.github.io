Best Victoria Sponge Cake Recipe

Read time: 5 Minutes

Cakes are a delightful way to celebrate life's sweetest moments, from simple gatherings to grand celebrations. The joy of baking a cake is in its ability to bring comfort, create connections, and share happiness with every slice. One of the most beloved cakes, the Victoria Sponge Cake, is a perfect blend of light, airy sponge and sweet, creamy filling, making it a timeless treat.

"Cake is happiness! If you know the way of the cake, you know the way of happiness! If you have a cake in front of you, you should not look any further for joy!" - C. JoyBell

Ingredients:

  • 200g unsalted butter, softened
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 100g strawberry jam
  • 150ml double cream, whipped
  • Icing sugar for dusting

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease two 20cm round cake tins.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Sift in the flour and baking powder, folding gently into the mixture.
  5. Stir in the milk until smooth, then divide the batter evenly between the two tins.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until golden and a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool the cakes in the tins for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once cooled, spread jam on one of the cakes, top with the whipped cream, and sandwich the cakes together.
  9. Dust with icing sugar before serving.
Enjoy the simple, classic joy of this beautiful sponge!
